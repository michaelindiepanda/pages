Artificial Intelligence Policy for Dateable

Last updated: October 15, 2025

1. Overview

Dateable is committed to transparency regarding the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in our platform. This policy outlines how we use AI technology to enhance your experience, protect our community, and facilitate meaningful connections while maintaining user privacy and safety.

2. How We Use AI

Dateable employs AI technology in various aspects of the platform to improve user experience and safety. Our AI systems are designed to support, not replace, human interaction and decision-making.

2.1. Matching and Recommendations

We use AI algorithms to:

  • Analyze user preferences and behavior to suggest compatible matches
  • Improve match quality over time based on user interactions
  • Personalize content and profile recommendations
  • Optimize the order of profiles shown to users

2.2. Content Moderation and Safety

Our AI systems help maintain a safe community by:

  • Detecting and flagging inappropriate content in photos and text
  • Identifying potentially harmful or abusive messages
  • Screening for spam, scams, and fraudulent accounts
  • Detecting fake profiles and catfishing attempts
  • Assisting in the review of reported content

Important: All content moderation decisions that result in account actions (warnings, suspensions, or bans) are reviewed by human moderators before being finalized.

2.3. User Experience Enhancement

AI helps improve your experience through:

  • Smart notification timing and personalization
  • Photo quality and profile completion suggestions
  • Conversation starters and icebreaker recommendations
  • Search and filter optimization

3. AI and User Privacy

Your privacy is paramount. When using AI systems:

  • We process your data in accordance with our Privacy Policy
  • AI processing is conducted securely and confidentially
  • We do not sell AI-processed insights to third parties
  • You maintain ownership of your content and data
  • AI models are trained on anonymized and aggregated data only

4. AI-Generated Content

Dateable currently does not generate profile content, messages, or photos using AI on behalf of users. Users are prohibited from:

  • Using AI-generated photos as profile pictures without disclosure
  • Representing AI-generated content as their own original work
  • Using AI chatbots or automated systems to interact with other users
  • Creating fake profiles using AI-generated identities

Violations of these prohibitions may result in account suspension or termination.

5. Limitations of AI

While we strive for accuracy, you should understand that:

  • AI matching algorithms are not perfect and cannot guarantee compatibility
  • AI content moderation may occasionally produce false positives or miss violations
  • AI recommendations are suggestions, not requirements
  • Human judgment remains essential in all relationship decisions

6. Human Oversight

Dateable maintains human oversight of all AI systems:

  • Critical decisions affecting accounts require human review
  • Users can appeal AI-flagged content for human evaluation
  • Our team regularly audits AI systems for fairness and accuracy
  • Customer support is always available for AI-related concerns

7. Fairness and Non-Discrimination

We are committed to ensuring our AI systems operate fairly:

  • AI algorithms are designed to avoid discrimination based on protected characteristics
  • We regularly test for and address algorithmic bias
  • Matching systems respect user preferences while promoting diversity
  • Content moderation applies consistent standards across all users

8. Data Used for AI Training

To improve our AI systems, we may use:

  • Anonymized interaction patterns and engagement data
  • Aggregated user preferences and behaviors
  • Content that has been reported or flagged for moderation review
  • General usage statistics and trends

We do not use your private messages or personal identifiable information for AI training without explicit consent.

9. Third-Party AI Services

Dateable may utilize third-party AI services and tools. When we do:

  • We carefully vet all third-party providers for security and privacy compliance
  • We ensure contractual protections are in place for your data
  • Third parties are prohibited from using your data for their own purposes
  • We remain responsible for AI processing performed by third parties on our behalf

10. Your Rights and Controls

You have the following rights regarding AI processing:

  • Request information about how AI affects your account
  • Appeal decisions made with AI assistance
  • Opt-out of certain AI-powered features (where technically feasible)
  • Request human review of AI-flagged content
  • Access your data as described in our Privacy Policy

11. Updates and Improvements

Our AI systems are continuously improved and updated. We may:

  • Add new AI features to enhance user experience
  • Refine algorithms based on performance and feedback
  • Adjust AI parameters to improve accuracy and fairness
  • Retire AI features that do not meet our standards

Material changes to how we use AI will be communicated through app notifications or email.

12. Reporting AI-Related Concerns

If you believe:

  • An AI decision affecting your account was made in error
  • Another user is using AI inappropriately
  • Our AI systems are operating unfairly
  • You have encountered AI-generated content that violates our policies

Please contact our Trust and Safety team through the app or at support@dateable.com.

13. Compliance with AI Regulations

Dateable is committed to complying with applicable AI regulations and laws, including:

  • Providing transparency about AI use as required by law
  • Conducting impact assessments for high-risk AI applications
  • Maintaining documentation of AI systems and their purposes
  • Cooperating with regulatory authorities as needed

14. Contact Information

For questions about this Artificial Intelligence Policy or how AI is used in Dateable, please contact us at ai-inquiries@dateable.com or through the app support feature.

15. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this AI Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or via email. Your continued use of Dateable after such modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
