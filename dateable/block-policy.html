User Suspension Policy

Last updated: October 11, 2025

All suspensions require a human review process.

1. Type 1: Inappropriate Photos/Bio

Suspension is conditional on resolving the content issue.

Violation Count Total Suspension Time Post-Resolution Restriction Resolution Deadline
First 7 days Remaining Suspension Time 30 days
Second 14 days Remaining Suspension Time 30 days
Third 30 days Remaining Suspension Time 30 days
Fourth Permanently Banned N/A N/A

Action Flow:

  1. User is suspended until the photo/bio issue is resolved.
  2. Once resolved, access remains restricted for the Remaining Suspension Time (Total Time - Time Served).
  3. If the issue is not resolved within 30 days, the user's user_status is set to paused.

2. Type 2: Inappropriate Messages/Behavior, Harassment

Suspension is time-based and must be served.

Violation Count Suspension Time
First 7 days
Second 14 days
Third 30 days
Fourth Permanently Banned

3. Type 3: Fake Profile/Catfishing, Spam/Scam, Under 18

Suspension is immediate and permanent.

Action: Permanently Banned

4. Appeal Process

If you believe your account was suspended in error or you wish to provide additional context, you have the right to appeal the decision.

To submit an appeal:

  • Open the Dateable app
  • Navigate to your profile
  • Under "Account status" copy the reference number and include it in an email to support@dateable.com
  • Provide a clear explanation of why you believe the suspension should be reconsidered

Appeals are reviewed by our Trust and Safety team within 5-7 business days. You will be notified of the outcome via the app and email.

5. Contact Information

If you have questions about our User Suspension Policy or need assistance with a suspended account, please contact our Trust and Safety team at support@dateable.com.

6. Policy Updates

Dateable reserves the right to modify this User Suspension Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated through the app. Continued use of the app after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
