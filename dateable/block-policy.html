Block and Suspension Policy for Dateable

Last updated: October 9, 2025

1. Overview

Dateable is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful community for all users. Our Block and Suspension Policy is designed to protect users from harassment, abuse, and other inappropriate behavior. When users repeatedly receive blocks from other members, this may indicate problematic conduct that requires review.

All suspensions require a human review process.

2. How Blocks Lead to Automatic Suspension

To ensure community safety, accounts may be automatically suspended when they reach certain block thresholds. These thresholds are designed to identify patterns of behavior that negatively impact other users.

Your account will be automatically suspended if:

  • You receive more than 10 blocks within 1 day
  • You receive 20 or more blocks within 3 days
  • You receive 30 or more blocks within 5 days

When your account is automatically suspended due to exceeding block thresholds, your account access is immediately restricted, your account status will show that you are suspended, and your account is assigned to a staff member for manual review.

3. Types of Violations and Suspension Policies

Suspensions can result from exceeding block thresholds or from specific policy violations identified during review. The type and duration of suspension depends on the nature of the violation.

3.1. Type 1: Inappropriate Photos/Bio

Suspension is conditional on resolving the content issue.

Action Flow:

  1. User is suspended until the photo/bio issue is resolved.
  2. Once resolved, access remains restricted for the Remaining Suspension Time (Total Time - Time Served).
  3. If the issue is not resolved within 30 days, the user's account is paused.

First Violation

  • Total Suspension Time: 7 days
  • Post-Resolution Restriction: Remaining Suspension Time
  • Resolution Deadline: 30 days

Second Violation

  • Total Suspension Time: 14 days
  • Post-Resolution Restriction: Remaining Suspension Time
  • Resolution Deadline: 30 days

Third Violation

  • Total Suspension Time: 30 days
  • Post-Resolution Restriction: Remaining Suspension Time
  • Resolution Deadline: 30 days

Fourth Violation

  • Action: Permanently Banned

3.2. Type 2: Inappropriate Messages/Behavior, Harassment

Suspension is time-based and must be served.

First Violation

  • Suspension Time: 7 days

Second Violation

  • Suspension Time: 14 days

Third Violation

  • Suspension Time: 30 days

Fourth Violation

  • Action: Permanently Banned

3.3. Type 3: Fake Profile/Catfishing, Spam/Scam, Under 18

Suspension is immediate and permanent.

Action: Permanently Banned

4. Staff Review Process

Once your account is assigned to a staff member, they will conduct a thorough review that may include:

  • Examining the reasons users blocked your account
  • Reviewing your profile content and messages
  • Checking for violations of our Terms and Conditions and Community Guidelines
  • Evaluating your overall conduct on the platform

The review process typically takes 3-5 business days. In complex cases, additional time may be required.

5. Appeal Process

If you believe your account was suspended in error or you wish to provide additional context, you have the right to appeal the decision.

To submit an appeal:

  • Open the Dateable app
  • Navigate to your profile
  • Under "Account status" click on "Suspension information" and follow the instructions

Appeals are reviewed by our Trust and Safety team within 5-7 business days. You will be notified of the outcome via the app and email.

6. Contact Information

If you have questions about our Block and Suspension Policy or need assistance with a suspended account, please contact our Trust and Safety team at support@dateable.com.

7. Policy Updates

Dateable reserves the right to modify this Block and Suspension Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated through the app. Continued use of the app after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
