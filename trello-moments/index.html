Important

Trello will make future updates to the structure that Trello Moments depends on to function properly. When it does, Trello Moments will stop working.

Most extensions rely on a structure that may change and I have no control over that.

I will do my best to make sure Trello Moments is updated and working in-line with any Trello updates.

Please don't use the Chrome Web Store review system as a way to contact me.

You will find I'm open to feedback and suggestions and working with you to make Trello Moments better.

If Trello Moments consistently stops working for you, and I am not aware of any update to Trello, please create a post on our Trello Plus community subreddit on Reddit.

Browser Compatibility

Trello Moments supports the following Chromium-based browsers:

  • Chrome
  • Edge
  • Opera
  • Brave
  • Vivaldi

All other browsers are not supported.

Features

Trello Moments is a Chrome extension that enhances your Trello experience by providing additional features and customization options. Here's how to use Trello Moments:

Popup

  • The extension's popup is located in your browser's toolbar. Click on it to open the popup.
  • The states are global, so they will apply to all Trello boards you are on.

Features

  • Customizable Categories: Choose from various image themes including emojis, food, and motivational content to match your style
  • Flexible Display Duration: Control how long each celebration appears on your screen
  • Custom Positioning: Place the celebration image exactly where you want it on your screen
  • Non-intrusive Design: Seamlessly integrates with your workflow while adding moments of joy
